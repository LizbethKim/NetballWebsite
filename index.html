Phoenix Karaka signs with Mojo Pulse

Phoenix Karaka, a 21 year old star defender from Invercargill has signed on with Mojo Pulse for the 2016 season in the ANZ Championship. Leaping out of the gates with a strong offensive style, Phoenix is bound to show off her talents and flexibility by being able to play in both the goal defense and goal keep positions, keeping her opponents guessing!
